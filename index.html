Welcome to Sree Narayana Sevika Samajam

A charitable organization situated at Thottumugham, Aluva, Ernakulam District, Kerala, India

About Us

Sree Narayana Sevika Samajam is a charitable organization established in 1965 under the Societies Registration Act, Travancore, 1955. Located on a hill in Keezhumadu, near Thottumugham, the institution is closely associated with Sree Narayana Guru and Sahodaran Ayyappan. The organization provides a home for orphans, destitute women, and elderly women. It also offers educational services and runs a library with over 30,000 books.
